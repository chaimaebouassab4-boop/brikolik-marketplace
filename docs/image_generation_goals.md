# Image Generation Goals (Homepage + Service Categories)

## Primary Goal
Generate professional demonstrative images for the home page and service categories, designed for a modern mobile startup app UI.

## Style Requirements
- Realistic and premium
- Bright natural lighting
- Clean modern homes and apartments
- Trustworthy and professional feeling
- Consistent visual style across all images
- Mobile app quality
- Elegant, clean, polished
- Moroccan / North African context where relevant
- No text, no logos, no watermark, no UI elements

## Output Specs
- Hero image: `assets/images/hero_handyman.png`
  - Orientation: landscape
  - Recommended ratio: 16:10
  - Suggested resolution: 1920x1200
- Service category images:
  - `assets/images/service_plumbing.png`
  - `assets/images/service_electrical.png`
  - `assets/images/service_cleaning.png`
  - `assets/images/service_painting.png`
  - `assets/images/service_gardening.png`
  - `assets/images/service_carpentry.png`
  - Orientation: square
  - Suggested resolution: 1024x1024

## Global Visual Direction
- Use one unified photographic language across all assets:
  - Natural daylight (soft morning or late-afternoon)
  - Premium residential interiors/exteriors (apartments, villas, terraces)
  - Warm neutral palette (beige, white, light wood, subtle earth tones)
  - Human-centered composition with clear service context
  - Clean framing and depth, realistic skin tones and materials
- Keep scenes documentary-realistic, not cinematic fantasy.

## Global Negative Constraints
- No text overlays
- No logos or brand marks
- No watermark
- No app UI, phone frames, mockup frames
- No exaggerated HDR, no surreal colors
- No cluttered or dirty environments
- No unsafe behavior (no sparks, no exposed danger)

- Is lighting natural and bright?
- Does the location look modern, clean, and premium?
